Problem Statement

Analyse the FINRA documents to find the factors that affect the decisions made by the judge.

Approach

  1. Extracted documents from FINRA website to gather corpus of text documents and metadata available at the website and extracted features relevant to the analysis.
  2. Performed regression modeling and statistical analysis to determine how various factors affect the decisions.

Results

Factors such as the type and number of representation, regions of the hearing sites, types of claims filed had impact on the outcome of the arbitration decisions.
