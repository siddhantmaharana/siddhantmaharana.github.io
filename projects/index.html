A Study of Amazon Review Data

An empirical analysis of the Amazon review data to find out interesting business trends using advanced SQL and data visualization. Used various machine learning models to extract sentiment of the reviews and develop analysis around the brand perception among the customers.Used text-mining methods to track fake reviews which could potentially lead to bias among the readers of the reviews. [MYSQL, text mining]

Bitcoin price prediction using Twitter sentiment
