Problem Statement

  1. Train a sentiment classifier based on the text of Forms 10-K,8-K.
  2. Compare the embedding layers trained in the classifier with another embedding layers trained using word2vec approach.

Approach

  1. Extracted text from the SEC documents to form the embedding layer.
  2. Trained a sentiment classifer model using LSTM and RNN networks and adjusted the parameters on the basis of the labels derived from the stock market price movements.
  3. Extracted the embedded layer from the above trained model.
  4. Designed an embedding layer based on the word2vec based training.
  5. Compared the two embedding layers and evaluate the better technique

Results

  1. The classifier did not perform optimally because of lower correlation of the price movements along with the SEC text information.
  2. The embedding layer designed using word2vec approach outperformed the one which was derived as a byproduct of the sentiment classifier.
